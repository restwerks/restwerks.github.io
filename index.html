How it works

AI handles it first.
You stay in control.

RestWerks connects to your clients over SMS through a dedicated number that represents your company. The AI handles support conversations around the clock, and you can jump in anytime from the same number.

1

Client texts in

Your client texts the number you give them. They talk like they text anyone. Casual, misspelled, incomplete. The AI understands all of it.

2

AI guides resolution

The agent knows your client's system. It walks them through the exact steps for their specific equipment, not generic instructions.

3

Resolved or escalated

Most issues close without tech involvement. Anything that needs a person gets escalated with full context so the tech walks in knowing exactly what happened.

4

You get a summary

Wake up to a clear record of every conversation. What happened, what was resolved, what needs follow-up. Everything logged automatically.

Your team can text clients from the same number too.

The AI handles the overnight volume, but your techs have full access to the same number during business hours. One consistent number your clients always recognize.

  • Send proactive maintenance reminders before problems happen
  • Follow up after a service visit from a number clients already trust
  • Announce upgrades, new products, or seasonal check-ins
  • Jump into any AI conversation manually when you want to
