How it works

Your AI twin handles it.
You review it in the morning.

RestWerks connects to your clients over SMS through a number that looks like yours. The AI handles the conversation from start to finish using real AV knowledge.

1

Client texts in

Your client texts the number you give them. They talk like they text anyone. Casual, misspelled, incomplete. The AI understands all of it.

2

AI guides resolution

The agent knows your client's system. It walks them through the exact steps for their specific equipment, not generic instructions.

3

Issue resolved or escalated

Most issues close without tech involvement. Anything that needs a person gets escalated with full context so the tech walks in knowing exactly what happened.

4

You get a summary

Wake up to a clear record of every conversation. What happened, what was resolved, what needs follow-up. Everything logged automatically.

The platform

Three products.
One platform that compounds.

RestWerks starts as an SMS agent and grows into a complete operating system for how you manage client relationships and system knowledge.

💬
RestText

The AI powered SMS support agent. Responds to clients 24 hours a day. Resolves the top issue categories without tech involvement. Escalates what it cannot handle.

🎫
RestTix

Every unresolved conversation becomes a structured ticket. Service notes feed back into the equipment profile automatically. Smarter than any generic ticketing system you have used.

📊
RestDash

A living view of every client household. Equipment profile, conversation history, service notes, and system photos all in one place. Institutional knowledge that never walks out the door.

🔘
RestPress

A physical button placed in the client home. One press opens a RestText conversation automatically. No number to find, no app to open. Just press and talk.
